Privacy Policy for Docs AI

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

Docs AI ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the Extension.

1. Information We Access

Google Docs Content

The Extension reads the text, headings, formatting, and document structure of the Google Document that you choose to work with in order to perform the requested editing and formatting actions.

Google Drive Files

If you paste an image into the Extension for insertion into a document, the image is uploaded to your own Google Drive (using your authorized Google account) so it can be inserted into the document.

User Commands

The Extension processes the natural language commands you enter (for example, "Make all headings bold and blue") to understand and perform your requested actions.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide the features of the Extension. Specifically:

  • Your document content and commands are sent to the Google Gemini API to interpret your requested actions.
  • Responses from the Gemini API are used only to perform the requested formatting or editing operation.
  • We do not use your document content, commands, or personal information for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.
  • We do not use your data to train our own AI models.

3. Data Processing and Storage

No Developer Data Storage

The Extension does not intentionally store your document contents, images, or chat history in developer-controlled databases.

Temporary Processing

Requests sent through our Cloudflare Worker are processed only for the time required to communicate securely with the Google Gemini API and return a response. The application does not intentionally retain document content after the request has been completed.

Local Storage

Authentication tokens and extension settings required for operation are stored locally within your browser's secure extension storage.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Docs API — Used to read and modify Google Documents with your authorization.
  • Google Drive API — Used to upload and insert images into your own Google Drive and Google Documents.
  • Google Gemini API — Used to interpret natural language commands and generate responses.
  • Cloudflare Workers — Used as a secure backend proxy to communicate with the Gemini API.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Docs AI's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Security

  • API keys are securely stored on the backend and are never embedded in the Extension.
  • Communication between the Extension, Cloudflare Workers, Google APIs, and the Gemini API is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Access to Google APIs is performed only after you grant the required permissions.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke the Extension's access to your Google account by visiting your Google Account permissions page.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised Effective Date.

9. Contact
