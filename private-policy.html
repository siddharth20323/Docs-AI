Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 July 2026

Docs AI (the “Extension”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the Extension.

1. Information We Access

To provide its functionality, the Extension may access the following information:

Google Docs content. The Extension reads the text, headings, formatting, and document structure of the Google Doc that you choose to work with in order to perform the requested editing and formatting actions.

Google Drive files. If you paste an image into the Extension for insertion into a document, the image is uploaded to your Google Drive using your authorized Google account so it can be inserted into the document. That image is made accessible by link so Google Docs can retrieve it for insertion.

User commands. The Extension processes the natural-language commands you enter, for example, “Make all headings bold and blue,” to understand and perform your requested actions.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide the features of the Extension. Your document content and commands are sent to the Google Gemini API to interpret your requested actions. Responses from the Gemini API are used only to perform the requested formatting or editing operation.

  • We do not use your document content, commands, or personal information for advertising, profiling, or marketing.
  • We do not sell Google user data.
  • We do not use your data to train our own AI models.

3. Data Processing and Storage

No developer data storage. The Extension does not intentionally store your document contents, images, or chat history in developer-controlled databases.

Temporary processing. Requests sent through our Cloudflare Worker are processed only for the time required to communicate securely with the Google Gemini API and return a response. The application does not intentionally retain document content after the request is completed.

Local storage. Authentication tokens and extension settings required for operation are stored locally within your browser’s secure extension storage.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension relies on Google Docs API to read and modify Google Docs with your authorization; Google Drive API to upload and insert images into your Google Drive and Google Docs; Google Gemini API to interpret natural-language commands; and Cloudflare Workers as a secure backend proxy to communicate with the Gemini API. Each service is governed by its own privacy policy and terms of service.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Docs AI’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Security

  • API keys are securely stored on the backend and are never embedded in the Extension.
  • Communication between the Extension, Cloudflare Workers, Google APIs, and the Gemini API is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Access to Google APIs occurs only after you grant the required permissions.

7. Your Rights

You remain in control of your Google account and data. You may revoke the Extension’s access in your Google Account permissions page, uninstall the Extension, and delete images uploaded by the Extension from Google Drive.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the Extension or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the Extension, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store Support page or the support contact provided with the Extension.
